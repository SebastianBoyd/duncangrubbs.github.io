About Me

Hi, my name is Duncan Grubbs. I am 16 years old and I am from the San Francisco Bay Area. I started photography after talking a photography class in the spring of 2015. My teacher, Mr. Martz inspired me to get more into photography then I ever had been before. After talking alot of photos. I felt like I needed a place where I could show them to anyone an anytime. Since I was already into computer programming, I decided it would be a good project for me to make a web-based photo portfolio. The webiste has gone through many different designs however I believe this is the best one yet.
