About Me

Hi, My name is Duncan Grubbs and I am an officer in the Drake Developers Club. Currently I am working on improving our club website as well as the home access client. I attend Sir Francis Drake High School in San Anselmo California. I am a junior this year and I am taking AP Computer Science as well as Physics and Chemistry. I love coding and web development and I really enjoy being a part of this club.

About the Club

The Drake Developers Club was founded by Lucas Martel, Jessica Gerwin, Zander Otavka, and myself last year. The mission of the club is to teach people how to code as well as create innovative projects that involve coding. We mainly focus on web design but have some projects in Python and Java. Each club member has thier own webpage that is linked to the main webpage. This way each member can show off his/her skills with web design. Our club meets every Wednesday in 127 (Mr. Milsteads room) for lunch. Feel free to join!

Join
